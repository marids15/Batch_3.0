Filene
Main_Program er hoved programmet
alt_1 og alt_2 er alternative løsninger som er mer rotete og være mindre oversiktlige. 
i den form at det er gjort forskjellige tilnærminger for å komme fram til funksjonaliteten.

Lineclip.css er css filen som er koblet opp til programmet.

User instructions:
 1. Draw lines in the canvas
 2. Click the “Check”-button to see if the lines are visible lines (lines inside the clipping window),
    invisible lines (lines that will just be cut as they do not cross into the clipping window),
    or a clipping case (these are lines that needs to be cut to see if they exist partially inside the clipping window).
    The lines will be listed from the newest on top to the oldest at the bottom.
 3. Click the “Clip”-button to cut the lines that are in the clipping case category,
    and thereby delete the invisible lines.
